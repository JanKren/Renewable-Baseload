
WIND + SOLAR COMBINED ANALYSIS - SUMMARY FOR PAPER
===================================================

Key Finding:
Adding solar PV to the European wind portfolio increases the guaranteed
baseload floor by 61% on average (range 11-106% across 2015-2024).

2024 Results (wind: 29, solar: 25, 8,783 hours):
- Wind-only baseload floor: 10.0 GW
- Combined (wind+solar) baseload floor: 16.7 GW
- Improvement: +6.7 GW (+67%)
- Wind-solar correlation: -0.33

The negative correlation (-0.26 average) indicates complementarity:
periods of low wind tend to coincide with elevated solar production.

Data Limitation:
ENTSO-E Transparency Platform captures 83% of official EU solar
generation (2023). The primary gap is Netherlands, where distributed rooftop
solar (~80% of 24 GW installed) is not reported to the TSO. Our combined
baseload estimates are therefore conservative.
